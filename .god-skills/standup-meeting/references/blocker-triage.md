# Blocker Triage

Use this reference when the standup is really about delivery friction rather
than a routine roll call.

## Minimum blocker fields

- blocked item
- blocker type
- owner
- next action
- when to escalate

## Good blocker language

- `missing-scope`
- `external-dependency`
- `environment-access`
- `review-needed`
- `approval-needed`
- `handoff-risk`
- `priority-conflict`

## Escalation reminders

### Escalate during or right after standup when

- no owner is obvious
- the blocker threatens the sprint or milestone goal
- another team or approver must act today
- work is piling up behind the same stuck item

### Keep it out of the standup when

- the blocker needs detailed debugging
- the issue is fundamentally a planning problem
- the team needs estimation, not coordination
- the right move is a retrospective process change, not same-day action

## Routing reminders

- If the team still needs to break down the work, route to `task-planning`
- If the real disagreement is about size or confidence, route to
  `task-estimation`
- If the issue is repeated process failure after the sprint, route to
  `sprint-retrospective`
